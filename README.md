# 📊 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance using Power BI, SQL, and Excel. The dashboard provides interactive insights into sales trends, customer behavior, regional performance, category contribution, product performance, and seasonality patterns to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* SQL
* Microsoft Excel

---

## Key KPIs

* Total Sales
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Unique Customers
* Category Contribution
* Segment Contribution
* Regional Performance
* Top Products

---

## Business Questions Answered

* Which region generates the highest revenue?
* Which customer segment contributes the most sales?
* Which product categories drive business performance?
* Are sales concentrated among a few products or diversified?
* What seasonal trends influence sales performance?
* Which regions show growth opportunities?

---

## Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest revenue contribution.
* Consumer segment accounted for more than 50% of total sales.
* West region achieved the highest overall sales performance.
* East region closely followed West, indicating strong growth potential.
* November and December recorded the highest sales activity.
* Revenue remained diversified across products and customers, reducing dependency on a single revenue source.
